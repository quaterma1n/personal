Take me HOM, country roads

Hello everyone, in this article I want to talk about what an abstract HOM (head of mobile) development department should do when starting development from scratch in an abstract mid-sized company. I have dealt with this task in companies of different sizes and levels of technological development, you can read more about it here Who I am. Why is it needed at all? - I have been using my notes on starting mobile development for many years, adding to them, and they have helped my friends find jobs, pass mobile system design interviews, and in general, so why not publish them.
What will be in this article
A high-level overview of all the topics and things you shouldn't forget at the start of mobile development
Life hacks, a large number of links to sources of information that I have used and continue to use in my work
Comparative analysis of tools and solutions, the results of which will help any head of mobile at the start of development
What won't be in this article
Advices like "you definitely need React Native / backend driven UI / GraphQL and so on when designing your pizza delivery app"
Deep diving into technology - I will try to give you a fishing rod, not give you fish.
No specific advice for a particular mobile platform - we will discuss mobile development regardless of devices and OS.
